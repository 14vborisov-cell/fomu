FOMU Account and Data Deletion

Users of FOMU can delete their account and request deletion of associated personal data in the following ways.

Delete your account in the app

You can delete your FOMU account directly in the app by going to:

Settings → Delete Account

Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, you can request deletion by emailing fomu.developer@gmail.com from the email address associated with your FOMU account.

Please include your:

  • FOMU username
  • email address or phone number linked to the account

We may ask for additional verification before processing the request.

Data that will be deleted

After a deletion request is submitted, FOMU will delete the account and associated personal data, including:

  • profile details
  • account identifiers
  • preferences and settings
  • data related to use of FOMU features

Deletion timeframe

Deletion is completed within 30 days after the request is submitted.

Data that may be retained

Some limited data may be retained where necessary for legal compliance, fraud prevention, security, or enforcement of legal obligations. Such data is retained only as long as necessary and then deleted or anonymized.
