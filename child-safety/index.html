FOMU Child Safety Standards

FOMU strictly prohibits child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in any form.

Users may not use FOMU to create, share, request, promote, or distribute content that sexually exploits or endangers children, including child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, sexual extortion, trafficking, or any other exploitative behavior involving minors.

Any attempt to use FOMU to harm, exploit, or endanger children is prohibited and may result in immediate removal of content, suspension or termination of the account, and reporting to relevant authorities where required by law.

When FOMU becomes aware of content or behavior involving CSAE or CSAM, we may investigate, remove violating content, suspend or terminate accounts, and take any other action required under our policies and applicable law.
