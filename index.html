FOMU

A digital platform for mutual interest.

FOMU is an online platform where users can submit the nickname or account of another person they are interested in. If two users submit each other, FOMU identifies that the interest is mutual inside the platform.

What FOMU does

FOMU is a digital social discovery product designed to help users identify mutual interest in a simple and private way.

  • Users can create an account.
  • Users can submit the nickname or account of another person.
  • Users can check whether the interest is mutual.
  • Users receive the result inside the platform.

How it works

1. Create an account
Users create a FOMU account to access the platform.
2. Submit a nickname or account
Users enter the nickname or account of another person they are interested in.
3. Mutual check
FOMU checks whether that person has also submitted them.
4. Result inside the platform
If both users have submitted each other, FOMU identifies mutual interest inside the platform.

Paid features

FOMU offers optional paid digital features and subscriptions.

  • The ability to submit additional nicknames or accounts.
  • Expanded account functionality.
  • Access to premium product features.
  • Enhanced access to tools available within the platform.

All paid services are digital and delivered online through the FOMU platform.
