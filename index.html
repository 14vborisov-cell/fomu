What FOMU does

FOMU is a social discovery app built around mutual vibe checks.

Users can add Instagram accounts they want to include, share a FOMU link through Instagram Stories, and reveal mutuals inside the app.

Core features

  • Create a FOMU account
  • Add Instagram usernames
  • Share a FOMU link on Instagram Stories
  • Let people choose you through the FOMU flow
  • Check whether the vibe is mutual
  • Reveal mutuals inside the app
  • Manage added usernames
  • Access optional premium features

How it works

1. Create an account
Users create a FOMU account to access the app and manage their vibe checks.
2. Add Instagram usernames
Users add Instagram usernames they want to include in their vibe check.
3. Share a FOMU link
Users can share their FOMU link through Instagram Stories. People who see the Story can open the link and choose the user through the FOMU flow.
4. Mutual vibe check
FOMU checks whether the vibe is mutual. A result is shown only when both people choose each other.
5. Reveal mutuals
If the vibe is mutual, the user can see the result inside the app. One-sided choices stay unrevealed.
