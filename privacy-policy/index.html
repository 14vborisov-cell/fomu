PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated May 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy for Club Room Limited (“we”, “us”, or “our”) explains how we collect, use, store, and share personal information when you use our mobile application Fomu and related services.

If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use Fomu. If you have any questions, you can contact us at fomu.developer@gmail.com.

1. What information do we collect?

Information you provide to us

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you use Fomu, create or manage your account, contact us, or interact with app features.

The personal information we collect may include:

  • Social media usernames or account names that you choose to provide, such as Instagram usernames;
  • Account information created inside Fomu;
  • Information you submit when contacting support, including your email address if you contact us by email;
  • Information connected with your app preferences, matches, mutual interactions, or other app activity.

Information automatically collected

When you use Fomu, we may automatically collect technical and usage information. This may include:

  • IP address;
  • Device type and device identifiers;
  • Operating system;
  • App version;
  • Log and diagnostic data;
  • Usage data, such as interactions with app features, screens, buttons, and subscription features.

This information helps us operate, secure, improve, debug, and analyse the app.

Push notifications

We may request permission to send you push notifications relating to your account, matches, mutual interactions, subscriptions, or other app features. You can turn off push notifications at any time in your device settings.

Subscriptions and payments

Fomu may offer paid subscriptions or premium features through in-app purchases. Payments are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not collect or store your full payment card details.

We may receive limited purchase-related information, such as subscription status, product identifier, renewal status, purchase history, transaction identifiers, and entitlement status, in order to manage access to paid features and provide support.

We may use RevenueCat to help manage subscriptions, verify purchases, and maintain subscription entitlements.

Sensitive information

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information, such as racial or ethnic origin, religious beliefs, health information, biometric data, or precise GPS location.

Social media usernames, not social login

Fomu may allow you to enter social media usernames, such as Instagram usernames, to enable matching and account features. Fomu does not require users to log in through Facebook, X, or other third-party social login providers.

2. How do we use your information?

We use your personal information to:

  • Create, maintain, and manage user accounts;
  • Provide Fomu’s matching, mutual interaction, and account features;
  • Process subscription access and premium features;
  • Verify purchases and subscription entitlements;
  • Send administrative messages, service updates, and push notifications;
  • Provide customer support;
  • Improve, debug, test, and analyse the app;
  • Protect the security and integrity of Fomu;
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, or misuse;
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. What legal bases do we rely on?

If you are located in the UK, EEA, Switzerland, or another region with similar privacy laws, we rely on the following legal bases where applicable:

  • Performance of a contract: to provide Fomu and paid features you request;
  • Consent: where you give permission, such as for push notifications or optional data use;
  • Legitimate interests: to improve, secure, analyse, and operate Fomu;
  • Legal obligations: where we need to comply with applicable law;
  • Vital interests: where necessary to protect someone’s safety.

4. When and with whom do we share your information?

We may share personal information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate Fomu. These may include:

  • Cloud hosting and database providers;
  • Analytics, logging, and crash-reporting providers;
  • Subscription and purchase-management providers, including RevenueCat;
  • Apple App Store and Google Play for in-app purchases and subscriptions;
  • Customer support or operational service providers;
  • Professional advisers, legal advisers, or authorities where required by law.

We may also share or transfer information in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, sale of company assets, or similar business transaction.

Fomu does not use an offer wall. We do not ask users to complete advertiser offers, surveys, app installs, or rewarded advertising tasks in exchange for virtual currency or rewards.

We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information for cross-context behavioural advertising.

5. How long do we keep your information?

We keep personal information only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

After your account is terminated or deleted, we aim not to keep personal information for longer than three (3) months, unless we need to retain limited information for legal, security, fraud-prevention, accounting, dispute-resolution, or backup purposes.

When we no longer need personal information, we will delete it, anonymise it, or securely isolate it until deletion is possible.

6. What are your privacy rights?

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you;
  • Request correction of inaccurate information;
  • Request deletion of your personal information;
  • Request a copy of your personal information;
  • Object to or restrict certain processing;
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent;
  • Appeal a decision where applicable under local law.

To exercise your rights, contact us at fomu.developer@gmail.com.

7. Account information

You may review or update certain account information through the app where available.

If you want to delete your account or request deletion of your personal information, contact us at fomu.developer@gmail.com.

8. Do-Not-Track features

Some browsers and devices include “Do-Not-Track” settings. There is currently no uniform industry standard for recognising or responding to these signals, so we do not currently respond to Do-Not-Track browser signals.
